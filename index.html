Rock Paper Scissors(RPS)



Choose Your Move:




How To Play Choose one of the moves to play.
The computer will choose random move.
Your goal is to hit 100 wins against the computer.
This is an easy game. If you lose too much, you are truly a fool.
Good Luck :).
